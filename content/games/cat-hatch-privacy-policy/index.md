# Privacy Policy for Cat Hatch: Idle Pet Game

**Effective Date**: May 16, 2026

## Introduction

OtherMythos ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Cat Hatch: Idle Pet Game application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

## Information We Collect

### Data We Do NOT Collect

Cat Hatch: Idle Pet Game is designed with privacy in mind. The App operates primarily offline and **does not**:

- Collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number
- Request your advertising ID
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or files
- Collect information about your device beyond what is necessary for the App to function
- Transmit gameplay data to external servers

### Limited Technical Data

The App may collect minimal technical information for purposes of:

- **App Performance**: Crash reporting and performance analytics (anonymised, no personal data)
- **App Store Services**: Required metadata for app store distribution
- **Non-Personalised Advertising**: Banner ads are served via Google AdMob using non-personalised ads only

This data is collected passively and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

## How We Use Information

Any technical data collected is used solely to:

- Improve App performance and stability
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Understand general usage patterns (aggregate data only)

## Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. The App stores all game data locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

## Advertising

Cat Hatch displays non-personalised banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads do not use personalised targeting and we do not request access to your advertising ID. The app does not track you for advertising purposes.

## In-App Purchases

The App offers a single non-consumable in-app purchase to disable banner advertisements. This purchase is handled securely through your device's app store and does not require us to collect personal data.

## Children's Privacy

Cat Hatch is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require account creation and does not collect personal data from any users.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party advertising networks or analytics services that collect personal data.

## Your Rights

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. Your game progress is stored entirely on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
